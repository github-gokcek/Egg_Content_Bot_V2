3️⃣ Stat sistemi (temel yapı)

Çok basit ama scalable bir sistem öneririm.

Base stats
STR  -> melee damage
DEX  -> crit + dodge
INT  -> magic damage
VIT  -> HP
AGI  -> speed
Derived stats
HP = 50 + VIT * 10
Mana = 30 + INT * 5

Attack = weapon + STR * 2
MagicAttack = staff + INT * 2

Defense = armor + STR
Speed = AGI
CritChance = DEX * 0.5%
DodgeChance = AGI * 0.3%
4️⃣ Combat matematiği

En kritik kısım.

Basit ama scalable formül:

Damage
damage = (attack - defense/2) * random(0.9 - 1.1)

Crit:

if rand < critChance:
    damage *= 1.75

Minimum damage:

damage = max(1, damage)
Dodge
if rand < dodgeChance:
    damage = 0
5️⃣ Level sistemi

En iyi XP formülü:

XP_to_level = 100 * level^1.5

Örnek:

level 1 -> 100
level 2 -> 282
level 3 -> 519
level 10 -> 3162

Bu exponential growth sağlar.

7️⃣ Raid sistemi

Raid için en iyi formül:

Boss HP:

HP = baseHP * playerCount * difficulty

Damage scaling:

bossDamage = base * (1 + raidSize * 0.15)
